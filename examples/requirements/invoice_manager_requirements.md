# Sample Requirements: Invoice and Payment Tracker

Build an invoice and payment tracking app for freelancers.

The system should allow users to create invoices with invoice ID, client name, client email, service description, invoice date, due date, amount, tax percentage, payment status, and notes.

The system should allow users to mark invoices as paid, update payment status, delete invoices, list all invoices, filter by client, filter by payment status, identify overdue invoices, calculate total billed amount, calculate paid amount, calculate outstanding amount, and generate a payment summary.

The system should prevent invalid emails, negative invoice amounts, invalid tax percentage, due dates before invoice dates, unsupported payment statuses, and updates to non-existing invoices.

The Gradio app should show invoice lists, filtered invoices, and overdue invoices as tables. Financial summaries should be displayed as readable Markdown metrics.
