# Sample Requirements: Office Asset Maintenance Tracker

Build an office asset maintenance tracking app.

Each asset should include asset ID, asset name, asset type, department, assigned employee, purchase date, warranty end date, condition, maintenance status, next maintenance date, and notes.

The system should add assets, update condition, assign assets, schedule maintenance, mark maintenance completed, list all assets, filter by department, filter by condition, identify assets due for maintenance, identify expired warranty assets, and generate asset health summary.

Validate dates, supported condition/status values, non-empty asset names, duplicate IDs, and non-existing asset updates.

The Gradio app should display assets, due maintenance assets, and expired warranty assets as tables. Health summaries should be Markdown reports.
