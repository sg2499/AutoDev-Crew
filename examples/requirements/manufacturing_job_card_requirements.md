# Sample Requirements: Manufacturing Job Card Tracker

Build a manufacturing job card tracking app.

Each job card should include job card ID, product name, batch number, quantity planned, quantity completed, machine name, operator name, start date, due date, status, and notes.

The system should create job cards, update completed quantity, update status, assign operator, list job cards, filter by machine, filter by status, identify delayed jobs, calculate completion percentage, and generate production summary.

Prevent negative quantities, completed quantity greater than planned, invalid dates, unsupported statuses, duplicate IDs, and updates to non-existing job cards.

The Gradio app should show job cards and delayed jobs as tables. Production summaries should be readable Markdown metrics.
