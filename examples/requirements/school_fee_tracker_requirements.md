# Sample Requirements: School Fee Tracker

Build a school fee tracking app.

Each fee record should include student ID, student name, class name, fee type, total fee, amount paid, due amount, due date, payment status, receipt number, and notes.

The system should add fee records, record payments, update payment status, list all records, filter by class, filter by payment status, identify overdue fees, calculate total collected, calculate total due, and generate class-wise fee summary.

Prevent negative amounts, amount paid exceeding total fee unless adjusted, invalid due dates, duplicate receipt numbers, and non-existing student records.

The Gradio app should show fee records, overdue fees, and filtered records as tables. Fee summaries should be Markdown metrics and tables.
