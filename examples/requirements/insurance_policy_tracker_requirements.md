# Sample Requirements: Insurance Policy Tracker

Build an insurance policy tracking app.

Each policy should include policy ID, policyholder name, policy type, premium amount, sum assured, start date, maturity/expiry date, payment frequency, status, nominee, and notes.

The system should add policies, update status, record premium payment, list policies, filter by policy type, filter by status, identify upcoming renewals, calculate total premium due, and generate policy summary.

Prevent negative premium/sum assured, invalid date ranges, empty policyholder names, unsupported statuses/frequencies, and updates to non-existing policies.

The Gradio app should show policies and upcoming renewals as tables. Policy summaries should be readable Markdown.
