# Sample Requirements: Recipe Cost Calculator

Build a recipe cost calculator app for small food businesses.

Each recipe should include recipe ID, recipe name, ingredients, ingredient quantities, ingredient unit costs, serving count, preparation time, category, and notes.

The system should add recipes, update ingredient costs, list recipes, filter by category, calculate total recipe cost, calculate cost per serving, identify high-cost recipes, and generate recipe profitability summary.

Prevent negative quantities/costs, zero servings, empty recipe names, duplicate IDs, and updates to non-existing recipes.

The Gradio app should show recipes and high-cost recipes as tables. Cost breakdowns and profitability summaries should be readable Markdown and summary tables.
