# Sample Requirements: Vehicle Service Tracker

Build a vehicle service tracking app.

Each vehicle should include vehicle ID, owner name, vehicle number, vehicle type, service date, service type, odometer reading, service cost, next service date, status, and notes.

The system should add service records, update status, update next service date, list service records, filter by vehicle type, filter by status, identify upcoming services, calculate total service cost, and generate service history summary.

Prevent invalid vehicle numbers, negative costs/odometer readings, next service before current service, unsupported statuses, and updates to non-existing records.

The Gradio app should show service records and upcoming services as tables. Service summaries should be readable Markdown.
