Build a support ticket management app for a small operations team.

The system should let users create tickets with title, customer name, priority, category, description, assigned agent, and status.

The system should allow ticket updates, status changes, priority filtering, assigned-agent filtering, open-ticket listing, ticket closure, and a summary of tickets by status and priority.

The Gradio app should display ticket lists and filtered results as tables. Ticket summaries should be displayed as readable reports or summary tables.
