# Sample Requirements: Warranty Claim Tracker

Build a warranty claim tracking app.

Each claim should include claim ID, customer name, product name, serial number, purchase date, claim date, issue description, claim status, service center, resolution notes, and replacement status.

The system should create claims, update claim status, update replacement status, list claims, filter by status, filter by service center, identify pending claims, calculate claims by status, and generate warranty service summary.

Prevent claim date before purchase date, empty issue descriptions, unsupported statuses, duplicate claim IDs, and updates to non-existing claims.

The Gradio app should display claims and pending claims as tables. Warranty summaries should be Markdown reports.
